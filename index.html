The Inversion

Every knowledge system ever built has been in the business of managing ! — the claim, the conclusion, the decree, the synthesis. Gutenberg made claims portable. Universities made them cumulative. LLMs made them universally accessible.

We are now at peak ! abundance. The scarce resource has shifted — not to better conclusions, but to the structure of reasoning that precedes them. Not what you think. How you got there.

That last transition has never happened before. Every human generation has re-derived its reasoning from scratch, inheriting only conclusions from the previous one. ? is the first infrastructure where how you reasoned compounds across time and across people.

What It Is

A zero-knowledge commitment layer for personal reasoning geometry. Not your conclusions. Not your content. The shape of your asking.

Inspired by Gärdenfors' conceptual spaces theory (2000) — the insight that cognition operates on geometric structures with defined quality dimensions, not symbolic logic and not pure neural blobs. You define the axes. The protocol commits to your placement on them. Content is never stored. Identity is never required.

decentralized ↔ centralized
open ↔ closed
long-term ↔ short-term
individual ↔ collective

The commitment is Poseidon(axis₀, axis₁, axis₂, axis₃, salt, domain). Computed client-side. Salt is private. Only the hash lands on-chain. Disclosure is voluntary, per commitment, irreversible.
